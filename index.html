Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Nigerian catering services

What types of dishes do you offer?

+

We specialize in authentic Nigerian cuisine including Jollof Rice, Egusi Soup, Pepper Soup, Fried Rice, Pounded Yam, Fufu, Ayamase, and much more. We also offer global fusion dishes and can customize menus for your preferences.

What are your serving sizes and pricing?

+

We offer three main serving sizes: Half Tray (serves 8-12 people), Full Tray (serves 15-20 people), and Custom sizes for larger events. Pricing varies by dish and quantity. Contact us at (470) 430-0782 for detailed pricing and custom quotes.

How far in advance should I place my order?

+

For regular orders, we recommend 24-48 hours notice. For large events and catering (15+ people), please order at least 3-5 days in advance. Holiday and special occasion orders may require more time.

Do you offer delivery and pickup?

+

Yes! We offer both pickup and delivery services throughout Atlanta and Gwinnett County. Delivery fees may apply based on location and order size. We also provide full-service catering with setup for events.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

+

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements. Please inform us of any restrictions when placing your order so we can prepare accordingly.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, Zelle, CashApp, and other digital payment methods. For large catering orders, we may require a deposit to secure your date.

Do you cater events and parties?

+

Yes! We specialize in catering for all types of events including birthday parties, family reunions, corporate events, weddings, cultural celebrations, and holiday gatherings. We can provide full-service catering with setup, serving, and cleanup.

How spicy is your food?

+

We prepare our dishes with authentic Nigerian flavors, which can be moderately spicy. However, we can adjust spice levels to your preference - from mild to extra hot. Just let us know your preferred spice level when ordering.

What makes your food authentic?

+

Chef T brings traditional recipes directly from Nigeria, using authentic spices, cooking techniques, and ingredients. Our dishes are prepared with the same methods passed down through generations, ensuring you get the true taste of Nigerian cuisine.

Can I see your food before ordering?

+

Check out our gallery section above to see photos of our dishes! You can also follow us on social media for regular updates. For large orders, we can arrange tastings upon request.
